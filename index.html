Maybe you've heard of mental conditions described as "chemical imbalances".
Sometimes, this is can be the case!
Similar to Goldilocks looking for a perfect bowl of porridge, too much or too little of certain brain chemicals can cause undesired effects.
Brains want the "just-right" amount of chemicals to function properly!
About the interconnectedness of the brain. (Assembly line of rats.)
Researchers work hard every day to understand how the brain works.
Although we don't have all the answers, we know that the brain and its networks
are intricately connected. They all need to be in sync to work properly!
